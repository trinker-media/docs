## Overview

The content calendar helps you plan, organize, and track your content publishing schedule. This guide covers the calendar interface, status indicators, and how to manage your content pipeline.

## Calendar Interface

### Viewing the Calendar

The content calendar displays your content in a calendar view:

- **Month view**: See all content scheduled for the month
- **Week view**: Focus on a specific week
- **Day view**: See detailed schedule for a single day
- **List view**: See all content in a list format

### Navigating the Calendar

- **Previous/Next**: Navigate between months, weeks, or days
- **Today**: Jump to today's date
- **Filter**: Filter content by status, integration, or keyword
- **Search**: Search for specific articles or keywords

## Status Indicators

The calendar uses status indicators to show the state of each article.

### Status Types

#### Draft

Articles that haven't been generated yet or are in progress.

**Indicator**: Gray or outlined icon

**Actions available**:
- Generate article
- Edit keyword
- Delete keyword

#### Generated

Articles that have been generated but not yet published.

**Indicator**: Yellow or orange icon

**Actions available**:
- Edit article
- Publish immediately
- Schedule publishing
- Delete article

#### Scheduled

Articles that are scheduled for future publication.

**Indicator**: Blue icon

**Actions available**:
- Edit article
- Edit schedule
- Publish immediately
- Cancel schedule

#### Published

Articles that have been published.

**Indicator**: Green icon

**Actions available**:
- View article
- Edit and republish
- View analytics (if available)

#### Failed

Articles that failed to generate or publish.

**Indicator**: Red icon

**Actions available**:
- Retry generation
- Retry publishing
- Delete article

## Tracking Content

### Upcoming Content

Track content that's planned or scheduled:

1. **View scheduled articles**: See all articles scheduled for future dates
2. **Check deadlines**: Monitor upcoming publication dates
3. **Review status**: Ensure articles are on track to be published
4. **Adjust schedule**: Reschedule articles as needed

### Completed Content

Track content that's been published:

1. **View published articles**: See all published articles
2. **Review performance**: Check how articles are performing (if analytics are available)
3. **Monitor updates**: Track which articles have been updated
4. **Analyze results**: Review which content performs best

### Content Pipeline

Manage your content pipeline:

1. **Plan ahead**: Schedule content weeks or months in advance
2. **Balance workload**: Distribute content creation evenly
3. **Track progress**: Monitor how many articles are in each status
4. **Identify gaps**: Find periods with no scheduled content

## Managing Content in the Calendar

### Adding Content

Add new content to your calendar:

1. **Add keyword**: Add a keyword that will become an article
2. **Generate article**: Generate an article from a keyword
3. **Schedule publishing**: Schedule when the article will be published

### Editing Content

Edit content from the calendar:

1. **Click on article**: Click on an article in the calendar
2. **Edit article**: Make changes to the article content
3. **Edit schedule**: Change when the article will be published
4. **Update status**: Change the article status if needed

### Rescheduling Content

Change when content will be published:

1. **Select article**: Click on the article you want to reschedule
2. **Edit schedule**: Change the publication date and time
3. **Save changes**: Confirm the new schedule

### Deleting Content

Remove content from your calendar:

1. **Select article**: Click on the article you want to delete
2. **Delete**: Confirm deletion
3. **Verify removal**: Ensure the article is removed from the calendar

## Common Issues

### Content Overlapping

**Problem**: Multiple articles scheduled for the same time.

**Solution**:
- Spread content throughout the week or month
- Adjust schedules to avoid overlaps
- Consider publishing frequency and capacity

### Missing Content

**Problem**: Gaps in your content calendar with no scheduled content.

**Solution**:
- Plan content in advance
- Use auto-publish to fill gaps
- Generate articles ahead of time
- Schedule content regularly

### Status Confusion

**Problem**: Unclear what status an article is in.

**Solution**:
- Review status indicators regularly
- Check article details for current status
- Update status manually if needed
- Contact support if status seems incorrect

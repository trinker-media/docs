## Overview

The Wix integration lets you publish articles from Genseo directly to your Wix Blog.  
Setup happens in two parts:

1. Install the **Wix Blog** app on your Wix site
2. Install and connect the **Genseo Wix integration** app

## Before You Start

- You need a Wix account with permission to install apps on the target site
- You need access to your Genseo dashboard
- If Wix Blog is already installed on your site, you can skip Step 1

## Connect Wix in Genseo

### Step 1: Install Wix Blog

1. Open the Genseo dashboard
2. Go to **Integrations**
3. Click **Connect Wix**
4. Click **Install Wix Blog App**
5. In the Wix Marketplace, click **Add to Site**
6. Select your Wix site and accept all requested permissions

Once completed, Wix Blog is installed on your site.

### Step 2: Install and Connect the Genseo Wix App

1. Open the Genseo dashboard
2. Go to **Integrations**
3. Click **Connect Wix**
4. Click **Install & Connect Wix**
5. In the Wix Marketplace, click **Add to Site**
6. Select your Wix site and accept all requested permissions
7. After the redirect back to Genseo, select your Wix site
8. Click **Connect to Wix**

Your Wix site is now connected to Genseo.

## Daily Auto-Publishing

Every morning, Genseo automatically publishes the post scheduled for that day.

## Troubleshooting

### Wix Site Does Not Appear in Genseo

- Make sure you selected the correct Wix account during app installation
- Verify you accepted all required permissions in Wix
- Refresh the Integrations page and try selecting the site again

### Connection Does Not Complete

- Close the flow and restart from **Integrations** → **Connect Wix**
- Confirm both apps are installed on the same Wix site:
  - **Wix Blog**
  - **Genseo Wix integration app**

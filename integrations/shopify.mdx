## Overview

The Shopify integration lets you publish articles directly to your Shopify store's blog. This guide covers installation, setup, publishing workflows, and troubleshooting.

## Installing the Genseo App

### Step 1: Install from Shopify App Store

1. Log in to your Shopify admin
2. Go to **Apps** → **App and sales channel settings**
3. Click **Shopify App Store**
4. Search for "Genseo"
5. Click **Add app**
6. Review permissions and click **Install app**

### Step 2: Grant Permissions

Genseo requires the following permissions:

- **Read and write blog posts**: To publish and update articles
- **Read store information**: To identify your store and blog

Click **Install** to grant these permissions.

## Connecting Shopify in Genseo

### Step 1: Authorize Genseo

1. After installing the app, you'll be redirected to Genseo
2. Click **Connect Shopify** if you haven't already
3. Authorize the connection

### Step 2: Select Your Blog

1. In Genseo, go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
2. Find your Shopify integration
3. Select which blog collection you want to publish to
4. Click **Save**

<Tip>
If you have multiple blogs in Shopify, you can connect multiple integrations or switch between them as needed.
</Tip>

## Publishing Articles to Shopify

### Immediate Publishing

1. Generate or open an article in Genseo
2. Click **Publish**
3. Select **Shopify** as the destination
4. Review the preview
5. Click **Confirm**

The article will be published to your Shopify blog immediately.

### Scheduled Publishing

1. Generate or open an article
2. Click **Schedule**
3. Select your publish date and time
4. Choose **Shopify** as the destination
5. Click **Schedule**

The article will be published automatically at the scheduled time.

## Updating Content

When you update an article in Genseo:

1. Make your edits in the Genseo editor
2. Click **Update**
3. Select **Update in Shopify**
4. Confirm the update

<Tip>
Genseo maintains a connection between articles and Shopify blog posts. Updates will overwrite the existing Shopify post.
</Tip>

## Common Issues and Solutions

### "App Installation Failed" Error

**Problem**: The Genseo app won't install in Shopify.

**Solution**:
- Ensure you have admin access to your Shopify store
- Check that your Shopify plan supports app installations
- Try installing from the Genseo dashboard instead

### "Blog Not Found" Error

**Problem**: Genseo can't find your Shopify blog.

**Solution**:
- Verify you have at least one blog created in Shopify
- Check that the blog is not hidden or deleted
- Ensure you selected the correct blog in Genseo settings

### "Publish Permission Denied" Error

**Problem**: Genseo can't publish to your Shopify blog.

**Solution**:
- Verify the Genseo app has write permissions
- Check your Shopify user permissions
- Try reinstalling the app with fresh permissions

### Articles Published But Not Visible

**Problem**: Articles are in Shopify but not showing on your storefront.

**Solution**:
- Check that your blog is enabled in your store theme
- Verify the article is set to "Visible" in Shopify
- Ensure your blog template is properly configured in your theme

### Formatting Issues

**Problem**: Article formatting looks wrong in Shopify.

**Solution**:
- Genseo publishes HTML content that should render correctly
- Check your Shopify theme's blog template styling
- Review the article in Shopify's editor and adjust if needed

### Image Upload Failures

**Problem**: Featured images aren't uploading to Shopify.

**Solution**:
- Ensure images meet Shopify's file size and format requirements
- Check your Shopify file storage limits
- Verify image URLs are accessible

## Best Practices

- **Test first**: Publish a test article before scheduling multiple articles
- **Review in Shopify**: Always check how articles appear on your storefront
- **Optimize images**: Compress images before uploading to improve load times
- **Monitor publishing**: Check your Shopify blog after publishing to ensure articles appear correctly

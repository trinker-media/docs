## Overview

The Shopify integration allows you to publish articles directly to your Shopify store's blog. This guide walks you through setting up the connection step by step.

<video controls className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl" src="https://videos.genseo.co/integrations/Shopify-Integration.mov" />

## Setting Up the Shopify App

### Step 1: Log in to Shopify

1. Log in to your Shopify admin panel

### Step 2: Enable App Development

1. Click on **Apps**
2. Click on **App and sales channel settings**
3. Click on **Develop apps**

### Step 3: Create App in Dev Dashboard

1. Click on **Build apps in Dev Dashboard** (you will be redirected to dev.shopify.com)
2. Click on **Create app**
3. Set the app name to **"Genese"**
4. Change the default app URL from `https://example.com` to `https://app.genseo.co`
5. Disable **"Embed app in Shopify admin"**

### Step 4: Configure Permissions

1. Select the following scopes:
   - **read_content**
   - **write_content**

### Step 5: Set Redirect URL

1. Set the redirect URL to: `https://app.genseo.co/api/integrations/shopify/callback`

### Step 6: Release the App

1. Click on **Release**
2. Confirm the release again

### Step 7: Set Up Custom Distribution

1. Click on **Home** in the left sidebar
2. Click on **Select distribution method** under **Distribution** on the right
3. Select **Custom distribution**
4. Enter your store URL (e.g., `MY-STORE-URL.myshopify.com`)
5. Click on **Generate Link**
6. Copy the link and open it in a new browser
7. Select your shop

## Connecting Shopify in Genseo

### Step 1: Connect Shopify in Genseo

1. Open Genseo and navigate to the **Integrations** page
2. Click **Connect** next to Shopify
3. Enter your shop URL (e.g., `MY-STORE-URL.myshopify.com`)

### Step 2: Copy Client ID and Secret

1. Go back to dev.shopify.com where you created the app
2. Select the app
3. Click on **Settings**
4. Copy the **Client ID** and **Secret**
5. Paste both values into Genseo

### Step 3: Save Configuration

1. Click on **Save Configuration**
2. Click on **Connect** or **Connect**

## Blog Configuration

### Field Mapping

After successful connection:

1. Select your blog under **Blog**
2. Map the following fields:
   - **Title** → Title
   - **Body** → Body
   - **Thumbnail** → Thumbnail
   - **Meta Description** → Excerpt (the meta description field must be connected to excerpt)
   - **Excerpt** → Excerpt

<Warning>
The keyword field is not supported by Shopify and cannot be mapped.
</Warning>

### Save Configuration

1. Click on **Save Configuration**
2. The connection is now successfully set up

## Publishing Articles

After successful setup, you can publish articles directly from Genseo to your Shopify blog:

1. Create or open an article in Genseo
2. Click **Publish**
3. Select **Shopify** as the destination
4. Confirm the publication

## Common Issues

### Connection Errors

**Problem**: Connection to Shopify fails.

**Solution**:
- Verify that the redirect URL is set correctly
- Ensure the Client ID and Secret were copied correctly
- Make sure the app was released in Shopify

### Permission Errors

**Problem**: Articles cannot be published.

**Solution**:
- Check that the scopes `read_content` and `write_content` are enabled
- Ensure the app is installed in your shop

### Field Mapping Issues

**Problem**: Fields cannot be mapped.

**Solution**:
- Make sure Meta Description is connected to Excerpt
- Note that the keyword field is not supported

## Exporting Shopify Products

To import your products into Genseo for content generation, you need to export them from Shopify first:

1. Open Shopify
2. Navigate to **Products** in the sidebar
3. Click **Export** in the top right corner
4. Under **Export**, select **All products**
5. Under **Export as**, select **CSV for Excel, Numbers, or other spreadsheet programs**
6. Click **Export** and download the file
7. Upload the downloaded file in Genseo settings

<Tip>
You can find the product upload option in **Settings** → **Integrations** → **Shopify** after connecting your store.
</Tip>

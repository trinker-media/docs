## Overview

Genseo offers multiple support channels to help you with questions, issues, and feedback. This guide covers how to access chat support, email support, and what to expect regarding response times.

## Chat Support

Chat support provides real-time assistance for quick questions and issues.

### Accessing Chat Support

1. **In-app chat**: Look for the chat icon in the Genseo app (usually in the bottom right corner)
2. **Click to open**: Click the chat icon to open the chat window
3. **Start conversation**: Type your question or issue and send your message
4. **Wait for response**: Wait for a support agent to respond

<Tip>
Chat support is typically available during business hours. Check Genseo's support hours for specific availability.
</Tip>

### When to Use Chat Support

Chat support is best for:

- **Quick questions**: Simple questions that need quick answers
- **Technical issues**: Immediate technical problems that need resolution
- **Account issues**: Account-related questions or problems
- **Billing questions**: Quick billing or subscription questions

### Chat Support Best Practices

- **Be specific**: Provide specific details about your question or issue
- **Include context**: Include relevant context (error messages, steps to reproduce, etc.)
- **Be patient**: Wait for a responseâ€”support agents may be helping multiple customers
- **Follow up**: Follow up if you don't receive a response within expected timeframes

## Email Support

Email support is available for more detailed questions and issues.

### Sending an Email

1. **Find email address**: Find Genseo's support email address (typically support@genseo.co or similar)
2. **Compose email**: Write a detailed email with your question or issue
3. **Include details**: Include relevant details (account information, error messages, steps to reproduce, etc.)
4. **Send email**: Send your email and wait for a response

### When to Use Email Support

Email support is best for:

- **Detailed questions**: Complex questions that require detailed explanations
- **Feature requests**: Suggestions for new features or improvements
- **Account issues**: Account-related issues that need detailed investigation
- **Billing disputes**: Billing or payment issues that need review

### Email Support Best Practices

- **Use clear subject lines**: Use descriptive subject lines that summarize your question or issue
- **Provide context**: Include relevant context and details in your email
- **Include screenshots**: Attach screenshots or screen recordings if they help explain your issue
- **Be patient**: Allow time for support to investigate and respond

## Response Times

Response times vary based on support channel and issue complexity.

### Chat Support Response Times

- **Typical response**: Usually within a few minutes to an hour during business hours
- **Business hours**: Faster responses during business hours
- **After hours**: Slower responses outside business hours
- **Complex issues**: May take longer for complex issues that require investigation

### Email Support Response Times

- **Typical response**: Usually within 24-48 hours
- **Business days**: Faster responses on business days
- **Weekends/holidays**: Slower responses on weekends and holidays
- **Complex issues**: May take longer for complex issues that require investigation

<Tip>
Response times may vary based on support volume and issue complexity. Check Genseo's support page for current response time estimates.
</Note>

Help support help you faster by providing:

- **Account information**: Your account email or username

## Common Questions

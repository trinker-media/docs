## Overview

Genseo helps you create SEO-optimized articles at scale by automating the content generation process. This page explains the core workflow and who the tool is designed for.

## The Genseo Workflow

Genseo follows a simple, four-step process to turn keywords into published content:

### 1. Keywords

Start by adding keywords that match your target audience's search intent. Genseo uses these keywords to understand what content to create and how to optimize it for search engines.

### 2. Generate Articles

Genseo generates complete, SEO-optimized articles based on your keywords. Each article includes proper headings, keyword placement, and structure designed to rank well in search results.

### 3. Edit

Review and refine the generated content to match your brand voice and ensure accuracy. You can edit articles directly in Genseo before publishing.

### 4. Schedule or Publish

Choose to publish immediately or schedule articles for future publication. Genseo can automatically publish to your Webflow or Shopify site.

## Who Is Genseo For?

Genseo is designed for:

- **Content teams** looking to scale their SEO content production
- **Marketing teams** needing to maintain consistent blog publishing schedules
- **E-commerce businesses** wanting to improve their search visibility with product-focused content
- **Agencies** managing content for multiple clients
- **Solo creators** who want to focus on strategy instead of writing

## Key Benefits

- **Save time**: Generate complete articles in minutes instead of hours
- **Scale content**: Produce more articles without proportionally increasing time investment
- **SEO-optimized**: Built-in SEO best practices ensure your content is structured for search engines
- **Integrated publishing**: Direct integration with Webflow and Shopify eliminates manual copy-paste
- **Content calendar**: Plan and schedule your content strategy in one place

## Overview

This guide walks you through creating and publishing your first article in Genseo. You'll connect an integration, add keywords, generate content, review it, and publish—all in a few minutes.

## Step 1: Connect an Integration

Before you can publish articles, connect Genseo to your website.

### For Webflow Users

1. Generate a Webflow API token in your Webflow account settings
2. In Genseo, go to **Settings** → **Integrations**
3. Click **Connect Webflow** and paste your API token
4. Select your site and map your CMS fields

<Note>
Need detailed instructions? See our [Webflow Integration guide](/integrations/webflow).
</Note>

### For Shopify Users

1. Install the Genseo app from the Shopify App Store
2. Grant the necessary permissions
3. Select your blog collection in Genseo

<Note>
See our [Shopify Integration guide](/integrations/shopify) for step-by-step setup.
</Note>

## Step 2: Add Keywords

1. Navigate to the **Keywords** section in Genseo
2. Click **Add Keyword**
3. Enter a keyword that matches your target audience's search intent
4. Click **Save**

## Step 3: Generate an Article

1. Go to the **Articles** section
2. Click **Generate Article** next to one of your keywords
3. Review the article brief and adjust settings if needed:
   - Tone of voice
   - Article length
   - Target audience
4. Click **Generate**

Genseo will create a complete, SEO-optimized article based on your keyword and settings.

## Step 4: Review the Article

1. Read through the generated content
2. Check for accuracy and alignment with your brand voice
3. Make edits as needed using the built-in editor
4. Verify SEO elements like headings and keyword placement

<Tip>
Don't skip the review step. Even AI-generated content benefits from human oversight to ensure it matches your brand and is factually accurate.
</Tip>

## Step 5: Publish

Once you're satisfied with the article:

1. Click **Publish** (or **Schedule** if you want to publish later)
2. Select your publishing destination (Webflow or Shopify)
3. Review the preview
4. Confirm publication

The article will be published to your site automatically.

## Common Issues

### Article generation fails

- Check that your keyword is specific enough
- Ensure you have available article credits
- Try regenerating with different settings

### Publishing fails

- Verify your integration is properly connected
- Check that required CMS fields are mapped correctly
- Ensure you have publish permissions in your Webflow/Shopify account
